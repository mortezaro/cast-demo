Why internal state is the next frontier

The same person isn't the same collaborator every moment.

Voice AI has learned to hear the words and hold a conversation. What it still misses is the human behind them — how much they can take in right now, how they feel, where they are. CAST reads that internal state live, and adapts.

CAST is our speech intelligence layer — a speech language model optimized for acoustic consistency, semantic grounding, and real-time speech generation. Developed in-house at 0.7B and 1.1B parameters.

01Speech-native language modelstrained on raw speech, not transcripts
02Speech-to-speech generationlisten and speak in one model, no separate ASR/TTS
03Interleaved speech + text modelscodec speech tokens and BPE text tokens in one stream
