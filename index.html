Why internal state is the next frontier

The same person isn't the same collaborator every moment.

Voice AI has learned to hear the words and hold a conversation. What it still misses is the human behind them — how much they can take in right now, how they feel, where they are. CAST reads that internal state live, and adapts.

Edge

Everyone races the same axis — full-duplex fluency. We add an orthogonal one: a continuous, interpretable human-state model that conditions generation. The two hard research questions — does speech carry recoverable cognitive state, can a small architecture beat scale — are already answered. What's left is build, not discovery.

Why a well-funded lab can't just buy this

  • Not on the internet. Paired speech + validated cognition can't be scraped — it has to be run as a study.
  • Longitudinal. The same people tracked across sessions over 10–12 days — and extendable — capturing within-person change no snapshot dataset holds.
  • Built-in clock. Protocol design plus longitudinal collection can't be compressed with budget. A lab starting today needs 6+ months minimum to stand up an equivalent.
Money doesn't buy calendar time. Our lead grows while they try to catch it.
Edge

The corpora are large but commodity. The asset that can't be scraped is a longitudinal, validated cognitive battery time-locked to each voice. Our published npj Digital Medicine work is the proof speech carries recoverable cognitive state.
